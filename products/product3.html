THE MPI 1K5

Category: Industrial Equipment

This is a portable and moveable Magnetic particle inspection Machine with 1500 Amp HWDC. It’s having option of use in AC current also.The unique parameter differentiating with others:

  • Fully copper power transformer and control transformer for durability and long life
  • Thyristor-controlled solid-state power control
  • Over-rated copper plates ensuring high current without heating
  • 90 sq. mm, 2.5-meter copper cable (one pair) with copper prods
  • Calibrated digital amp meter showing actual current in AC or HWDC mode
  • No instant calibration control switch ensuring rigid power accuracy
  • Single-touch demagnetization using Mag/Demag toggle switch (AC decay operation)
  • Built-in electronics with high-quality spares for long-term operation
  • Stepless infinite current control for various inspection jobs
  • Complies with ASTM E1444/E and other applicable standards
  • Rear-side two-pole MCB main switch
  • Auxiliary 220V AC output for UV light usage
